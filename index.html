Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive about 5–10 minutes early so you have time to check in, settle in, and enjoy a relaxed start to your appointment.

Can I get a massage if I have an injury?

If you have an injury, recent surgery, or a medical condition, please consult your doctor first and let us know before your session begins.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please call as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment so the time can be made available for another guest.

Is there parking available?

Parking is generally available near the location. Please allow a few extra minutes during busy hours.
