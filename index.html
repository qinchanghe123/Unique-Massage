FAQ

Helpful details before you arrive.

A few simple notes to help your visit feel smooth, comfortable, and easy.

Please arrive a few minutes before your appointment so you can settle in calmly and begin your visit without feeling rushed.

If you have an injury, recent surgery, serious pain, or a medical condition, please consult a qualified medical professional before booking. You may also call us with general comfort preferences before your visit.

Please call as soon as possible if your plans change. Advance notice helps us keep appointment times available for other guests.

Parking availability may vary by time of day. Please check the plaza parking area when you arrive or call ahead if you need help locating the store.
